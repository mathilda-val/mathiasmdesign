What It Does

At trade shows, salespeople meet dozens of prospects a day. Business cards pile up. Half get lost, the other half sit in a jacket pocket for weeks before someone manually types them into the CRM. By then, the lead is cold.

This automation eliminates that gap. A salesperson snaps a photo of a business card in Telegram ‚Äî or just types the person's details ‚Äî and within seconds:

  • An AI agent reads the card via OCR and extracts every field
  • The card image is uploaded to HubSpot's file manager and backed up to Google Drive
  • A contact is created in HubSpot with all fields populated
  • A detailed note (in German) is created and pinned to the contact ‚Äî with the business card attached
  • The lead is logged to a Google Sheet with a direct HubSpot link
  • A confirmation message is sent back in Telegram

The AI Layer

Both paths (photo and text) use GPT-4o-mini as an AI agent with a detailed system prompt. The agent doesn't just extract data ‚Äî it determines salutation from the name (Herr/Frau), reads handwritten notes on forms, translates captions from the salesperson, and writes structured notes formatted for the sales team. The AI calls the sub-workflow as a tool, passing all extracted fields directly into HubSpot.

Why It Matters

Average time from "met at a trade show" to "in the CRM with notes and card attached" dropped from 3‚Äì5 days to under 30 seconds. No lost cards. No forgotten leads. No "who was supposed to follow up?" Every lead is captured, documented, and actionable immediately.

Built in 2024‚Äì2025 with n8n ‚Äî before any AI tool had training data on the platform. Pure documentation reading, trial and error, and stubbornness.
