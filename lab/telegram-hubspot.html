What It Does

At trade shows and networking events, salespeople meet dozens of prospects a day. Business cards pile up. Half get lost, the other half sit in a jacket pocket for weeks before someone manually types them into the CRM. By then, the lead is cold.

This automation eliminates that gap entirely. A salesperson snaps a photo of a business card in Telegram ‚Äî or just types the person's details ‚Äî and within seconds, the contact exists in HubSpot with all fields populated, a sales owner assigned via round-robin, and a follow-up task created. The bot confirms everything right in the chat.

How It Works

  • Telegram trigger watches for incoming messages in the sales team's bot
  • Photo path: Downloads the image ‚Üí runs OCR to extract name, email, phone, company ‚Üí cleans and structures the data
  • Text path: Parses typed contact info directly
  • Sub-workflow handles the CRM logic: deduplication check ‚Üí create or update ‚Üí round-robin assignment ‚Üí task creation
  • Confirmation sent back to Telegram with the contact name and assigned salesperson

Why It Matters

The average time from "met at a trade show" to "entered in CRM" dropped from 3-5 days to under 30 seconds. No more lost cards. No more forgotten leads. No more "who was supposed to follow up with that person from the booth?" Every lead is captured, assigned, and actionable immediately.

Built in 2024 with n8n ‚Äî before any AI tool had training data on the platform. Pure documentation reading, trial and error, and stubbornness.
